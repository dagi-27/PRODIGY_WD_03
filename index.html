Tic Tac Toe

🎮 Tic-Tac-Toe is a timeless classic where two players battle to align three symbols in a row! Whether vertically, horizontally, or diagonally - the first to achieve this wins! Perfect your strategy and outsmart your opponent in this battle of wits! 🌟

📜 How to Play:

  1. Player 1 starts with ❌ (X)
  2. Player 2 uses ⭕ (O)
  3. Alternate turns placing your symbol
  4. First with 3 in a row wins! 🏆
  5. If the board fills up with no winner, it's a draw! 🤝

📚 Game Rules & Strategy

🏆 Master the game with these pro tips:
  • Control the center square for better chances
  • Create multiple winning possibilities
  • Block your opponent's potential lines
  • Watch for fork opportunities

⚡ Quick Rules

  1. X always moves first
  2. Players alternate turns
  3. No move changes allowed
  4. First to 3 in a row wins
